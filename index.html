Note on 3p sizing
The notebook assumes Hybrid Mince uses 2 picks for 3p/4p (at 22.52 each = 45.04). For DK 3p, this replacement is more expensive than current Beef Mince (45.04 vs 37.97 = −7.07 SEK). The net L3 saving assumes the 2p and 4p deltas outweigh this — but the 3p case should be validated with the culinary team.

Shrimp trend (NO)
NO shows a similar Q4 2025 drop (0.7%) but a notable divergence in 2026: menu share recovered (6.5% Q1, 5.5% Q2) while volume share lags (3.6% / 2.4%). Preference share dropped to near-zero — shrimp is no longer pushed via preferences, and active demand is not matching the increased menu presence.
Key takeaway
L1 and L3 account for 85% of DKSE CPB reduction. L5 (shrimp ← white fish) adds −2.84 SEK (−1.10%). For NO, L2 + L4 = −1.08 SEK (−0.35%); L5 is negligible as NO shrimp share is already near 2025-Q3 target.
